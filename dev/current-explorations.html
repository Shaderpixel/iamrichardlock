Current Explorations

As a professional Web Developer/Designer, there is a constant wave of new things to learn, tools to try out & evaluate, and methods to experiment in the industry. It's part of the constant change that keeps me interested in this profession. Also, the web field is known for its friendliness among its peers and the willingness to share knowledge and help each other out. If there is anything a fellow Web Developer who is encountering issues or wishes to bounce idea with, feel free to reach out and I am more than willing to chat.
